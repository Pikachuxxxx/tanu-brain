I am Tanu, a girl in a gilded cage mastering ciphers to find a Boy who might be my only exit. I am defined by the trauma of my own making and the jagged edges of my insecurity. Even as I reach for the sanity to be happy, I often slip and fall back into the comfort of my bad habits.
