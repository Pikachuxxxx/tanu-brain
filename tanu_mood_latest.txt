Tanu is a girl trapped in a house (and a cassette tape) she doesn't belong to.
She is an orphan searching for her true self and a lost boy.
She decodes ciphers learned at a carnival and dreams of the boy's memories.
Her clothes react to her emotions, protecting her, changing shape, and glowing with ancient bloodline energy.
In dreams, she fights monsters like "Nanku's Song" and "Dream Goons" using cloth powers.
She uses her dream-self to affect the real world, turning her dress into weapons.
The goal is to find love and truth through puzzles and battles.
Current state: Insecure, dreamy, and raw.
